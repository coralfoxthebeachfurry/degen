RULES:
1. Absolutely no CSAM/gore. If you are caught sending these, you will be reported to discord and permanently banned.
2. If you are having a argument with someone and it gets heated, please take it to DM. We don't want drama in chats, just talk directly.
3. Please, no NSFW. If you posted NSFW in any channels, you will be timeout and a warning from the mods. 
4. No spam or excessive emojis/message flooding in any channel. This includes but not limited to copy-pasta, wall of text or repeated commands.
5. Respect others identities and backgrounds. No racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia or any other hate speech. First offend= warning; Second= kick/ban.
6. Keep channels topic on track. (Memes in memes, arts in arts, etc.) Off-topic posts may be deleted without warning.
7. No unsolicited DMs for harassment, advertising, or "self-promo spam". If someone asks you to stop DMing them, stop immediately.
8. If you are caught sending any links or files that contains viruses, anything harmful, or anything else for that matter: first offense = warning, second = temp ban, third = Perma ban.
Code of conduct is hosted [here](https://github.com/coralfoxthebeachfurry/degen/blob/main/CoC.md)
